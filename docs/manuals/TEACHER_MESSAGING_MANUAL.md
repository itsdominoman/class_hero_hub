# Teacher messaging manual

## Purpose and boundaries

CHH Messages is for school-authorised communication. Use the conversation already provided by assignments and school policy. Do not create alternate records outside CHH to bypass access, closure, or retention rules. For immediate danger, use the school's emergency process.

## Daily workflow

1. Confirm the active school and role, then open **Messages**.
2. Confirm the student/family/staff context before writing.
3. Keep text factual and suitable for a school record.
4. Review photos for unrelated children, documents, or location detail. CHH strips image metadata but cannot correct inappropriate content.
5. Record voice notes in a quiet place and listen before sending.
6. Use urgency only under school policy.
7. Interpret Sent/Delivered/Read literally; absence of Read is not proof that a person ignored a message.

Assignments and participant access are revalidated on every protected action. If access ends, do not ask another staff member to forward content. Report a mistaken recipient, concerning content, or unexpected access to the school's safeguarding/operations contact and preserve the conversation reference.

## Troubleshooting

- **Read-only/closed:** follow the school contact route; do not repeatedly retry.
- **Photo/voice unavailable:** preserve the message reference and report it. Do not upload the same sensitive content to an unapproved service.
- **Message submitted twice:** client identifiers make normal retry idempotent; refresh the timeline before sending again.
- **No notification:** the message may still be committed. Check the timeline; notifications are a prompt, not the system of record.
