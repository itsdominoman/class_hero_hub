# Troubleshooting

## Who this is for
Parents, guardians, and support helpers.

## 1) Cannot log in
- **Problem:** You cannot get into the app.
- **Likely reason:** You are on the wrong Google account, or your access has not been approved yet.
- **What to try:** Use **Continue with Google**, confirm the Google account, and reload the page.
- **When to contact support:** If it still fails after trying again.

## 2) Google login confusion
- **Problem:** You are not sure where to enter your password.
- **Likely reason:** Google sign-in happens on Google’s secure page, not inside Family Hero Hub.
- **What to try:** Choose **Continue with Google** and sign in on the Google page.
- **When to contact support:** If the Google page never opens or you keep returning to login.

## 3) Access request pending
- **Problem:** You asked for access but still cannot get in.
- **Likely reason:** The request has not been approved yet, or the email does not match your Google sign-in.
- **What to try:** Submit the request again only if needed, and double-check the email address.
- **When to contact support:** If the request stays pending for a long time.

## 4) Child link expired
- **Problem:** The child sees an expired link message.
- **Likely reason:** The QR code or link has timed out.
- **What to try:** Generate a fresh QR code or link and open it again on the child device.
- **When to contact support:** If new links still expire or fail.

## 5) Child device not opening
- **Problem:** The child device does not open the dashboard.
- **Likely reason:** The link is incomplete, expired, or the device is offline.
- **What to try:** Check internet access, use the full link or QR code, and confirm the correct device.
- **When to contact support:** If the device still will not open the child dashboard.

## 6) Reward request missing
- **Problem:** A reward request does not appear for the parent.
- **Likely reason:** The child sent it from the wrong device, or the page has not refreshed yet.
- **What to try:** Check the child dashboard, open **Review reward requests**, and refresh the page.
- **When to contact support:** If the request still never appears.

## 7) Points look wrong
- **Problem:** The child’s point balance does not look right.
- **Likely reason:** A recent add points, remove points, savings deposit, savings unlock, or reward request changed the balance.
- **What to try:** Open the points log and check recent point actions, savings deposits, savings unlocks, reward requests, approvals, and rejections.
- **When to contact support:** If the log does not explain the balance change.

## 8) Calendar item missing
- **Problem:** A task or event is not showing.
- **Likely reason:** The wrong child is selected, the item is inactive, or the date is wrong.
- **What to try:** Select the right child and check the date, repeat rule, and active status.
- **When to contact support:** If the item still does not appear.

## 9) School Bag wrong
- **Problem:** School Bag shows the wrong items.
- **Likely reason:** The wrong child or weekday is selected, or the school item was entered incorrectly.
- **What to try:** Check the child, weekday, and school item entry.
- **When to contact support:** If the wrong items keep showing.

## 10) Phone or browser layout issue
- **Problem:** The page looks broken, cramped, or hard to tap.
- **Likely reason:** The browser needs a refresh, or the screen size is causing layout trouble.
- **What to try:** Refresh the page, try a different browser, or rotate the phone and rotate it back.
- **When to contact support:** If the layout still looks broken after trying again.

## 11) When to contact support
Contact support if:
- you cannot log in after trying again
- access approval seems stuck
- a child link keeps failing
- points or reward requests do not match what you expect
- the issue still happens after refresh and re-login

## Quick support note
If a problem keeps coming back, save a screenshot and the exact error text before you contact support.
