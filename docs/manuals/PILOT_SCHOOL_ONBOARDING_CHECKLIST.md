# Messaging v1 pilot-school onboarding checklist

## Governance and policy

- [ ] Named System Owner and backup active administrator confirmed.
- [ ] Owner meaning, transfer, emergency recovery, and staff offboarding understood.
- [ ] Safeguarding reviewer/access-manager/export/legal-hold grants approved.
- [ ] School contact hours, urgent-use rule, receipts, and parent reply policy approved.
- [ ] Retention values and legal-hold procedure reviewed under local policy/law.
- [ ] Incident lead, privacy contact, safeguarding lead, and technical support path recorded.

## Technical preparation

- [ ] Student, teacher, assignment, guardian/FHH link, dual-role, and school scope samples verified.
- [ ] Android package, development endpoint, signature and app-link/deep-link behavior verified.
- [ ] Staff/parent logout and account switching tested.
- [ ] Text/photo/voice, receipts, contact-hours release, push direction, invalid token, and FHH School Chats smoke tests passed.
- [ ] Safeguarding review/isolation, restriction/closure, flag/note, export verification, and legal hold tested.
- [ ] Worker heartbeat, dead-letter/replay, disk/database/backup health and migration revision green.
- [ ] Encrypted database/media backup and disposable restore rehearsal current.

## People and launch

- [ ] Teachers trained with practice conversations.
- [ ] Parents receive School Chats purpose, contact-hours, notification, privacy, and emergency guidance.
- [ ] Safeguarding team completes a supervised practice case and export verification.
- [ ] System Owner practices staff invite, access review, preview, and handover.
- [ ] Limited cohort, launch date, success measures, support coverage, pause/rollback criteria agreed.
- [ ] iOS limitation and no Play/App Store publication understood.
