# Quick Start for Children


## What to do first

1. Open your child dashboard.
2. Look at your points.
3. Check your rewards.
4. Look at My Day.
5. Check School Bag.

## How to use it

- **Points** show how much you have earned.
- **Rewards** are things you can ask for.
- **My Day** shows what you need to do today.
- **School Bag** shows school things to remember.

## Ask for a reward

1. Choose a reward.
2. Tap **Request**.
3. Wait until it says **pending**.
4. Ask your parent if you are unsure.

## If you get stuck

- Ask your parent.
- Ask for a new link if the device stops working.
- Refresh the page if it looks frozen.


## Quick reminder

Have fun, earn points, and ask your parent if you need help.
