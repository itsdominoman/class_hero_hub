# Safeguarding-access manager manual

Access is explicit, school-scoped, revocable, and independent of a staff role. Apply least privilege:

- `Review` opens time-limited evidence reviews.
- `Moderate` applies protected interventions.
- `Export evidence` requests/downloads packages.
- `Export internal notes` adds the more sensitive note layer.
- `Manage legal holds` places/releases confidential preservation holds.
- `Manage safeguarding access` grants/removes these permissions.

Verify the person's identity, active membership, school, business need, start/end expectation, and manager approval. State a meaningful reason. Do not grant permissions to a shared account. Review grants on staff changes and at a school-defined cadence. Removal blocks new actions; historical audit remains.

The System Owner receives access-management and legal-hold-management grants at bootstrap so they can establish the initial team. They do not automatically receive review/export powers; those remain explicit.
