# Market Research Summary

## Competitor Landscape

### Money-First / Fintech
- **Greenlight**, **GoHenry**, **BusyKid**, **FamZoo**, **Spriggy**, **RoosterMoney**, **Bankaroo**, **Step**
- *Focus:* Allowance, debit cards, financial literacy.

### Behaviour / Chores / Family Management
- **OurHome**, **S’moresUp**, **Joon**, **Homey**
- *Focus:* Chore tracking, routine building, gamification.

## Market Desires
- **Less Nagging:** Parents want systems that run themselves.
- **Reduced Chaos:** Clear expectations and fair rewards.
- **Simple Setup:** Low barrier to entry for busy families.
- **Visible Progress:** Kids need to see they are moving forward.
- **Trustworthy Logs:** Accurate history of points and money.

## Child Psychology / Motivation
- Immediate feedback is crucial.
- Responded well to points, stars, and coins.
- Gamification elements: badges, streaks, milestones, evolving avatars/pets.
- Missions/Quests provide a sense of purpose.

## Positioning: Family Hero Hub
**"From Reminders to Responsibility"**
Family Hero Hub positions itself as the **Family Command Center**. It combines the best of chore tracking and financial learning with a hero-themed journey that turns household tasks into adventures.
